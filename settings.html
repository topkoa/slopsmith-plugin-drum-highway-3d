3D Drum Highway — Mockup

Visual prototype. Renders an 8-lane drum highway populated from a hardcoded demo pattern that loops indefinitely. Not yet wired to song data — pick any song in the player, then switch the viz to "3D Drum Highway" to see the mockup animate.

HH SNR TM1 TM2 FT CR RD KCK

The fill showcase is the best read of how ghost notes, accents, flams, and ride-bell hits look on the highway.

0 = looking down the lanes (low angle). 1 = top-down. Drums generally benefit from a steeper angle than guitar — you're reading 8 horizontal positions, not 6 frets at depth.
